# Commercial Definitions

Commercial definitions for sales, marketing, and business development teams. These are canonical templates usable by any organization to drive revenue and partnerships.

## Purpose

Commercial definitions are **input** for commercial teams to produce:
- Customer relationships and pipelines
- Marketing campaigns
- Partnership agreements
- Brand assets

## Categories (by L1 Value Model Layer)

### Business Development & Partnerships
Strategic partnerships, alliance management, collaboration models.

### Brand & Positioning
Brand identity, market differentiation.

### Sales & Marketing
Lead generation, campaign execution, customer retention.
