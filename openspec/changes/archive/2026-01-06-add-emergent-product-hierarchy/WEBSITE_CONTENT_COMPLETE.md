## Progressive Disclosure Strategy (Implemented)

### Layer 1: Vision (Landing Page)

**Audience:** Everyone  
**Goal:** Establish philosophical foundation  
**Outcome:** Visitor understands the paradigm shift (static → adaptive)  
**Next Step:** Choose pathway (Builders → Tech Stack, Leaders → Solutions)

### Layer 2: Hub Pages (Tech Stack / Solutions Overview)

**Audience:** Segmented (Builders vs. Leaders)  
**Goal:** Apply vision to audience-specific context  
**Outcome:** Visitor understands how principles apply to their domain  
**Next Step:** Deep dive into specific product

### Layer 3: Product Detail Pages (emergent.core / emergent.product)

**Audience:** Highly targeted (Developers vs. Product Leaders)  
**Goal:** Comprehensive value proposition with features, use cases, pricing  
**Outcome:** Visitor ready to take action (demo, docs, GitHub, contact)  
**Next Step:** Conversion (sign up, request demo, explore GitHub)
